--- layout: post title: "Sulu gets an asteroid" tags: ["asteroids", "asteroids", "george takei", "george takei", "heroes", "heroes", "sci-fi", "sci-fi", "space", "space", "star trek", "star trek", "sulu", "sulu", "television", "television", "tv", "tv"] --- George Takei, better known as Sulu to the world of non-hardcore Trek fans, now has an asteroid named after him. I say non-hardcore Trek fans because hardcore Trek fans know his real name. Or maybe it's semi-hardcore. Super hardcore probably have lost all touch with reality and believe he really is Sulu. In any case, asteroid 7307 is now 7307 Takei, joining the mighty ranks of 4659 Roddenberry and 68410 Nichols (Lt. Uhura's real name). In an unfortunate bit of news, the asteroid was knocked off a balcony roof in New York and fell to its death.
