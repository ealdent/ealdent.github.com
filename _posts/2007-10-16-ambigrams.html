Erin McKean, the Dictionary Evangelist, has a post today on the coinage of the term ygology, meaning the study of palindromes. Her take is that the word was an inevitability of English, demanding birth. No argument there. But then she also references ambigrams, which are visual representations of words that are somehow symmetric. If you flip them upside down, they form the same word.
